Platform One

The VRChat Campus

DeSchool's early home is a fully realized school environment inside VRChat — Asian-school inspired architecture with layered improvements over the pre-alpha. Walk the halls, sit at a desk, pick up an object, and class is in session.

  • 📺
    YouTube on classroom screens — curated educational content embedded live in the world
  • 🖥
    School computers in VRC port directly to Socratic Social — the web and the world are one
  • 🎒
    Interactable school objects — books, tools, boards — built by 3D engineer Kandi River
  • 👥
    Human-hosted classes run alongside AI sessions — real instructors, real community
Platform Two

The Socratic Social Platform

The web layer of DeSchool lives at socratic-social.com — a dialogue-first social learning platform engineered by Eric Trotchie. Not a content dump; a community built around depth, equity, and real exchange.

  • 💬
    Socratic dialogue at the structural core — questions drive the curriculum
  • 🔗
    Bridged from VRChat — what happens on campus flows to the platform and back
  • ✅
    Verified educators and mental health professionals woven into the experience
  • ⚖
    Normalization by design — every voice, every background, no gatekeeping

How It Works

The DeSchool Method

01

Enter the Campus

Join the VRChat world or log into Socratic Social. The campus is always open — no schedule required to explore, though live classes run on set times.

VRChat + Web
02

Sit in on a Session

ONI and human instructors run ongoing classes. Screens play curated YouTube content, the whiteboard is live, and every desk object is interactive.

Live & Async
03

Join the Dialogue

Take the conversation to Socratic Social — post, respond, push back, go deeper. The platform is designed to reward critical thinking, not just engagement metrics.

Socratic Social
04

Connect with Professionals

Verified educators and mental health professionals are embedded in the community — available for structured sessions, not just passive posting.

Verified Network
05

Build and Contribute

The campus grows. New rooms, new instructors, new objects — the world expands with the community. Propose content, host a class, shape DeSchool's future.

Community-Built
06

Carry It Forward

Learning doesn't stop at logout. Everything discussed, shared, and built on the platform persists — your progress, your conversations, your growth.

Persistent Progress
