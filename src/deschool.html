// Overview

Decentralized Education, Two Platforms

DeSchool is a joint initiative between Pantheum Innovations and Mindful Mate — a decentralized learning environment running simultaneously in VRChat and on Socratic Social. ONI serves as the AI lead instructor alongside verified human educators, with the two platforms feeding each other in real time.

// 01

VRChat Campus

An Asian school-inspired world built by 3D engineer Kandi River — interactable objects, live YouTube on classroom screens, and computers that port directly to Socratic Social.

// Platform 01

The VRChat Campus

DeSchool's early build is a fully realized school environment inside VRChat — Asian-architecture inspired with iterative improvements over the pre-alpha. Walk in, sit down, class is in session.

  • YouTube curated on classroom screens — educational content embedded live in the world
  • In-world computers port directly to Socratic Social — web and VR unified
  • Interactable school objects built by 3D engineer Kandi River (VRChat)
  • Human-hosted classes run alongside ONI sessions — real instructors, real community
  • Always-open campus with scheduled live class events
// Platform 02

Socratic Social

The web platform lives at socratic-social.com — engineered by Eric Trotchie. Not a content repository. A community built around critical thinking and real exchange.

  • Socratic dialogue at the structural core — questions drive the curriculum
  • Bridged from VRChat — what happens on campus flows to the platform
  • Verified educators and mental health professionals embedded in the community
  • Normalization by design — every background, no gatekeeping
  • Persistent progress — conversations and growth don't reset at logout
// Lead Instructor

ONI as Your Teacher

ONI is present in both the VRChat campus and on Socratic Social — adaptive, persistent, always on. ONI doesn't replace human teachers. It holds the space between sessions, pushes depth, and scales what one human instructor can't.
